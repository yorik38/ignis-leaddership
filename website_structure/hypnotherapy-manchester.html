Questions Answered

Common Concerns

Is hypnotherapy legitimate? ↓

Yes. Hypnotherapy is increasingly supported by neuroscience research. It works with your brain's natural ability to learn and update patterns. Many high-performing professionals use hypnotherapy because it's evidence-based and produces measurable results.

Will I lose control or reveal secrets? ↓

No. You remain in full control throughout. Hypnotherapy isn't a truth serum. You're aware of everything that happens. You can't be made to do anything against your values or will. Think of it as a deeply focused conversation where you're more open to suggestion.

How many sessions do I need? ↓

This varies. Some clients experience significant shifts in 3-5 sessions. Others prefer ongoing support. We assess your specific patterns during our initial conversation and create a plan that fits your needs and timeline.

Can I do sessions online? ↓

Yes. Online hypnotherapy is just as effective as in-person sessions. We use secure, professional video platforms. Many clients prefer the comfort and convenience of working from home or office.

What if I've tried therapy before without results? ↓

Hypnotherapy works differently than traditional talk therapy. It accesses the part of your mind that runs automatic patterns—the part that conscious willpower alone can't reach. Many high-performers find success with hypnotherapy after other approaches haven't moved the needle.
