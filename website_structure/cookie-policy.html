1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. Similar technologies can include scripts, tags and local storage. They can help a site function properly, remember preferences, improve performance and measure usage.

2. How we use cookies

We may use cookies to keep the site secure, remember essential preferences, understand how visitors use the site, and support embedded tools such as scheduling widgets. Some cookies may be set by third parties whose services appear on the site.

3. Strictly necessary cookies

Some cookies are essential for the operation of the website or a service you actively request. These do not normally require consent under PECR, but we still explain them here for transparency.

4. Analytics and performance cookies

If we use analytics or similar performance cookies that are not strictly necessary, we will ask for your consent before placing them on your device. If you decline, those cookies should not be set.

5. Third-party tools

This website may use third-party services such as Calendly for booking. Such services may set their own cookies or similar technologies when loaded. Their use of those technologies is governed by their own policies.

6. How to manage your preferences

You can manage cookies through your browser settings and, where available, through our cookie controls. Blocking some cookies may affect how the site or booking tools function.

If you have any questions about cookies on this website, contact yorik@ignisleadership.com.
