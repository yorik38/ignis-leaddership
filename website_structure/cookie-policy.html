Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function, remember preferences, and understand how visitors use pages.

1. Cookies that may be used

  • Essential cookies to support core website functionality
  • Performance or analytics cookies to understand page visits, usage patterns, and site improvements
  • Functional cookies to remember preferences where relevant
  • Third-party cookies from embedded tools such as booking widgets, video platforms, or social links

2. How cookies are used

Cookies may be used to improve navigation, understand how the website performs, support embedded services such as scheduling tools, and make the experience more useful over time.

3. Managing cookies

You can control or remove cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect how parts of the website function.

4. Third-party tools

Where third-party tools are used on the site, such as booking or analytics tools, those providers may place cookies in line with their own policies and settings.

5. Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in tools, services, or legal requirements. The latest version on this website applies.
