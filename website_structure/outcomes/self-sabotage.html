What Self-Sabotage Costs You

You Sabotage Just Before Winning

Success feels dangerous. Right when breakthrough is close, you find a way to derail yourself.

You Downplay Your Achievements

Deep down, you feel like a fraud. You fear exposure. Success triggers anxiety, not joy.
