Ignis Leadership respects your privacy and is committed to handling personal information responsibly. This policy describes how information may be collected through the website, contact forms, bookings, email correspondence, and delivery of services.

1. Information we may collect

Depending on how you interact with the business, this may include your name, email address, phone number, company details, enquiry details, session notes, booking information, and any other information you choose to share.

2. How information is used

  • To respond to enquiries and arrange consultations

3. Lawful basis

Information may be processed where it is necessary for pre-contract steps, performance of a contract, compliance with legal obligations, legitimate interests, or consent where consent is the appropriate basis.

4. Sharing of information

Personal information is not sold. It may be shared with trusted service providers where reasonably necessary to operate the business, such as booking, payment, website hosting, or email tools. Information may also be shared where required by law or where there is a serious safeguarding concern.

5. Retention

Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purpose it was collected, to meet legal and accounting obligations, and to maintain appropriate client records.

6. Security

Reasonable administrative, technical, and organisational steps are taken to protect personal information. However, no method of online transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to access, correct, restrict, erase, or object to the processing of your personal information, and in some cases to request portability.

8. Cookies and analytics
