## Outcome Page Template (Conversion-Focused Structure)

### 1. HERO SECTION (above fold)
- **Problem headline** (speaks directly to their pain)
- **Subheading** (positions the solution, outcomes-focused, not method-focused)
- **Hero image/visual** (professional, relatable)
- **Primary CTA**: "Let's Talk" or "Book a Strategy Call"

**Example for "Perfectionism":**
```
HEADLINE: "Perfectionism That Once Drove You Is Now Holding You Back"
SUBHEADING: "The Clear Identity Method helps high-performing leaders finally separate
their worth from their output—so you can achieve without the constant self-criticism."
CTA: "Let's Talk"
```

### 2. PROBLEM IDENTIFICATION (relatable section)
Headline: "Do you relate to this?"

4-5 specific symptoms/manifestations (bullet-style, relatable language)

**Example for "Perfectionism":**
- Rewriting emails 10 times before sending
- Feeling your worth fluctuate with each project outcome
- Unable to celebrate wins—already thinking about what's next
- Saying yes to everything, then burning out
- Judging yourself by standards you'd never hold others to

### 3. THE COST (emotional/business impact)
Show what happens if they don't solve it:
- Energy drain
- Relationship strain
- Career ceiling (can't delegate, can't rest)
- Constant self-doubt despite external success

**Headline**: "The Hidden Cost of Staying This Way"

### 4. WHAT BECOMES POSSIBLE (outcome-focused benefits)
Don't say "You get coaching + hypnotherapy"
Say "You'll experience..."

**Example:**
- Celebrate wins without immediately doubting them
- Make decisions and move forward—no second-guessing
- Feel your worth independent of outcomes
- Rest without guilt
- Lead teams without burning out

### 5. HOW IT WORKS (brief process overview)
Very light touch on method. 2-3 sentences max.

**Example:**
"The Clear Identity Method uses cognitive coaching and neuroscience-based techniques to rewire how your mind evaluates success. It's not another self-help framework. It's about changing the internal dialogue that's been running unconsciously for years."

### 6. SOCIAL PROOF (testimonial)
1 relevant testimonial (choose one that specifically mentions this outcome)
Include: Name, Role/Context, Quote

### 7. INVESTMENT & CTA (pricing/closing)
Headline: "Ready to Shift This?"

Copy:
"Investment starts from £897, tailored to your specific situation and goals.

During a short consultation, we'll identify exactly where you're stuck and design a path forward that fits your life and budget."

**Primary CTA**: "Let's Talk" (links to booking)
**Secondary info**: "No pressure. No upsell. Just clarity."

### 8. CROSS-LINKS (to related outcomes)
"Related transformations you might explore:"
- Link to 2-3 related outcomes (e.g., if on "Perfectionism" page, link to "Burnout" and "Decision-Making")

## Copy Tone & Style for Ignis Leadership

**Vibe**: Relaxed but professional. Warm but direct. Successful but not arrogant.

**DO:**
- Use "you" language (speak to them)
- Be specific about what changes (not vague promises)
- Acknowledge their success (they're already winning externally)
- Name the unnamed problem (e.g., "success without peace")
- Short sentences. Breathe. Simple words.

**DON'T:**
- Use jargon ("cognitive behavioural hypnotherapy for executive presence")
- Make it about method ("Our proprietary Clear Identity Method™")
- Over-promise ("Transform in 6 weeks!")
- Sound salesy ("Don't miss out!")

**Example of RIGHT tone:**
"You perform. You excel. But something's missing—ease. Peace. The sense that you're enough."

**Example of WRONG tone:**
"Unlock your full executive potential through our proprietary neuroscience-backed methodology."
