I spent over a decade leading large teams inside some of the UK's most demanding organisations. Director level. Board visibility. Real pressure. And through most of it, I carried a voice that questioned whether I truly belonged there.

That voice didn't go away when I got promoted. It didn't quiet when projects succeeded. If anything, the stakes rising made it louder. I was performing confidence I didn't feel: exhausted, wired at 3am, and unable to switch off.

The work I now do with clients is the work I had to do on myself. Executive coaching opened the conscious layer. Hypnotherapy did what coaching alone couldn't. It went deeper, faster, and changed the story I was telling myself at the root.

I built Ignis to give high-performers access to both. Because you deserve to lead from the real version of yourself.

I also spent a decade in high-pressure corporate roles, so I understand the world you're navigating. I'm not teaching theory. I'm teaching what actually works, because I've had to do it myself.
